The experience

A three day immersive music experience in the realm between reality and fiction, always showcasing fantastic talent and jaw dropping production.

Following the huge success of its first ever performance in 2020. Sohl Music Festival is bringing back the fenomenal three-day music festival experience on July 29-31 2022. Welcoming the most spectacular group of entertainers within electronic dance music along with an audience from all corners of the globe united again as one in one place – celebrating the spectacle of relationships.

We offer you an adventure with multiple stages all with their own individual theme. From last times success story with our mainstage covered in tropical vibes we are now launching brand new themes all to reflect the wonders of the world- a surprising epic journey through the worlds corners. Sohl Music Festival is primarily a place to share new experiences, recharge your batteries and make friends for life. We promise you a show that is seldom seen, a gathering of talents that left you in amazements and wonder.

Frequently asked questions

Here we have collected everything you need to know to make the most out of your festival experience.

Opening times and dates

The festival arena is open 15:00 - 00:00 CEST July 29 to 31.

What's the minimum age to attend Sohl Music Festival?

You have to be at least 18 years old to enter the festival, so make sure to bring a valid ID.
No exceptions are possible.

Location

This years edition will be held at Avicii Arena in Stockholm, Sweden

Where can I park my car?

There is a designated parking lot near the festival.
Parking tickets are €14,99 and only available in the ticket shop.

What items are not allowed on the festival site?

Drones,
Illegal substances,
Portable laser equipment and pens,
Any item which may reasonably be considered for use as a weapon,
No bags larger than A4 size can be brought into the arena,
Gas canisters,
Nitrous oxide canisters,
Alcohol,
Food,
(there is food available in the arena, provided by our independent food vendors)

What's the price for a ticket?

€79 for a day ticket and €149 for a weekend pass

Can I purchase a ticket for just one day?

There is a very limited amount of single day tickets available for Friday, Saturday and Sunday. However, the festival is designed as a weekend experience, so we recommend you to join the festival for a full weekend.

I'd like to upgrade my day ticket to a weekend ticket, is that possible and what should I do?

That is not a problem, you can contact our ticketing partners at tickster.com to help you with an upgrade.

Can I buy a ticket at the entrance?

There are no tickets available at the entrance. You can only get a ticket in pre-sales.
